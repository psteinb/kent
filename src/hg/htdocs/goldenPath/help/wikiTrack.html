Description

The Wiki track displays genome annotations added by the Genome Browser user community. These annotations have not been curated.

As of June 2012, this track is a read-only track. Annotations are still available and viewable in the track and all annotations can be viewed at the Genome Annotation wiki page, but no new annotations are being accepted and existing annotations can no longer be freely edited.

Policies

  • The UCSC Genome Browser group reserves the right to remove any annotations.
  • Annotations are not curated by UCSC.
