UCSC Genome Browser: Site Down for Maintenance
 

This UCSC Genome Browser site is currently down for maintenance. Please check our genome-announce mailing list for the latest information about outages. If you have any questions or issues, you can reach us at genome-www@soe.ucsc.edu.

You are welcome to continue using any of our other official sites, although your saved sessions and custom tracks may not be available:

  • genome.ucsc.edu
  • genome-euro.ucsc.edu
  • genome-asia.ucsc.edu

In addition, some third-party mirrors are available for viewing most of the more recent assemblies on the UCSC Genome Browser site:

  • genome-mirror.cshl.edu - provided by the Simons Center for Quantitative Biology at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory (CSHL). This mirror may contain additional CSHL-generated annotation in addition to the standard UCSC tracks. Many thanks to Adam Siepel at CSHL for providing this mirror.
