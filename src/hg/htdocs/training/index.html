Getting Help with the UCSC Genome Browser
 
 

Host a Genome Browser Workshop!       Map

Timeslots are available to host a Genome Browser workshop at your institution. Thanks to the funding support of NHGRI, we offer hands-on Genome Browser training onsite at your institution, tailored to your audience's level of expertise.

For more information or to submit a request to host a workshop, please visit our signup.
